Method / 02

How a CRIT job runs.

Predictable work comes from predictable process. Four stages, every project — from a 10-drop tenant fit-up to a campus refresh.

01

Site Walk

We meet on-site, map closets, riser paths, ceiling space and constraints — then quote against reality.

02

Design

Drawings, BOM, labeling scheme and a clear scope. No surprises mid-pull.

03

Install

Trained techs, neat trays, J-hooks at code, fire-stopped penetrations. The work is the portfolio.

Every run tested with Fluke, every drop labeled, every panel documented. You get the binder.
